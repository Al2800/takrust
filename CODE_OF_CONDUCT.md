# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a collaborative, respectful, and technically rigorous
community for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in technical discussion.
- Assume good intent and focus on evidence-based reasoning.
- Give actionable, specific feedback.
- Keep security-sensitive details private until maintainers advise disclosure.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing or publishing private information.
- Disruptive conduct intended to derail collaboration.
- Bad-faith security disclosures.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and
other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to maintainers. Include context links,
timestamps, and a short description of observed behavior.

All reports are reviewed in good faith and handled with discretion.
